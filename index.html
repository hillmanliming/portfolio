Studio branding

As a group of four members, we settled on the name 'Compass' because it encompasses each original member and the various directions we can go with our creativity. After settling on the name I initialized a word association web for the word 'compass'. From here we went to the direction of boats, and exploration. Finally we settled on a ship/boat with a shadow, together forming the North and South needle.

In figma we started designing our studio's website. I designed the first home and contact page. After Bjorn and I finalized the design, I coded the header and the footer.
